nft marketplace

We are building a simple-to-use NFT Marketplace for the everyday person.
We are building to allow various applications that might want to use our plaform for browsing/discovery and management of NFTs, including Wallets or DAO frameworks for example. It was built for that very purpose.

Our NFT marketplace implements a royalty standard for minted NFTs, as well as for externally minted NFTs. This enables NFTs sold on protocol applications to be purchased and sold on other protocol applications, while still adhering to creator and platform royalties.

OUR METAVERSE

KATARA – THE KINGLY PRIESTS

Join the Kingly Priests and watchers of the Multiverse, a collection of 50 rare NFTS immortalized on Binance Smart Chain, each with their unique Universal Wisdom and with a collective mission to protect the Multiverse from the Apostate Dragons. Each Kingly Priest grants access to the exclusive member’s only club, KATARA-NIVEH: Home of the Gods.
Kataras live their lives on the brink of the multiverse, not actively getting involved, but close enough to observe. Though they’re inherently devoid of any shape or form, here on Planet Earth, they usually choose to appear as human Kings with elements of their godly forms. While they’re completely peaceful, their sheer existence is like an eerie reflection of humanity, a constant awkward, inquisitive stare that makes one squirm with fear and awe.
